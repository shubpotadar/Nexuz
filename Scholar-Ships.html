What is the NEXUZ Scholarship?

The NEXUZ Scholarship is divided into 2 International Distinction Awards. They will be offered to two future international students, who will prove their potential of transforming the world into a better place. The International Distinction Awards have a total value of 15,000 EUR, with the big prize being 10,000 EUR, and the small one being 5,000 EUR.

About NEXUZ

NEXUZ gives international students the possibility to explore all their study options in one place. NEXUZ also empowers the world to choose education. How? By gathering all the relevant information for future international students like yourself and using it to develop informative and user-friendly websites and tools.

How to Apply


1. Register

Register in one of our portals, subscribe to emails from your "Account Settings" page and answer a few questions about yourself!

2. Explore

Explore our portals and use our features to find the best degree for you

3. Apply

Apply to the university (and the programme) of your dreams

4. Submit

Submit your Letter of Acceptance and Letter of Motivation (400 - 600 words)

5. Win

The winners will be revealed on the 1st of August 2022, announced via email and other social media channels!
